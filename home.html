WHAT IS NATURE

Nature is an inherent character or constitution, particularly of the ecosphere or the universe as a whole. In this general sense nature refers to the laws, elements and phenomena of the physical world, including life.

the FOUR Elements

The ancient Greeks believed that there were four elements that everything was made up of: earth, water, air, and ice. This theory was suggested around 450 BC, and it was later supported and added to by Aristotle
